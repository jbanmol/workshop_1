## Long Description

CleanBrowse is a powerful, lightweight, and privacy-respecting ad blocker built for the modern web. Designed exclusively for Chrome with the latest Manifest V3 technology, it offers a faster, cleaner, and more secure browsing experience by blocking intrusive ads, malicious trackers, and annoying cosmetic elements.

**Why Choose CleanBrowse?**

- **High-Performance Blocking:** By leveraging Chrome's native `declarativeNetRequest` API, CleanBrowse blocks content with minimal impact on your browser's performance. Pages load faster, and your CPU and memory usage stay low.
- **Privacy is Paramount:** We believe your data is yours. CleanBrowse does not track your browsing history or collect any personal information. Our commitment to privacy is transparent and unwavering.
- **Simple & Intuitive UI:** Manage your settings with ease. The CleanBrowse popup lets you quickly see blocked items, whitelist a site with a single click, or access the full options page for more control.
- **Fully Customizable:** Take control of your browsing experience. Add your favorite sites to the whitelist, import custom filter lists, and fine-tune your settings to match your needs.
