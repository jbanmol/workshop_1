# Privacy Policy for CleanBrowse

**Last Updated:** [Date]

Welcome to CleanBrowse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in the CleanBrowse Chrome extension.

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect

We believe in privacy by design. CleanBrowse is built to be a privacy-first application, which means we **do not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or browsing data. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Your browsing history, URLs, or search queries.
- Any data you enter into web forms.
- Your IP address or location information.
- Any information that could be used to personally identify you.

## 2. Data We Collect (Only for Premium Subscribers)

If you choose to purchase a CleanBrowse Premium subscription, we collect a minimal amount of information required to process your payment and manage your license. This includes:

- **Email Address:** We collect your email address during the checkout process to send you your license key and communicate important account-related information.
- **Payment Information:** All payments are processed by our third-party payment provider, Stripe. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. We only receive a confirmation of your payment and subscription status from Stripe.
- **License Key:** We generate and store a license key associated with your subscription to validate your premium status.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

The limited data we collect from premium subscribers is used solely for the following purposes:

- To process your payment and activate your premium subscription.
- To verify the status of your license key.
- To send you important updates about your subscription or the CleanBrowse extension.
- To respond to your support inquiries.
